Orange Cap

The Orange Cap is awarded to the highest run-scorer in the Indian Premier League (IPL) each season. It is one of the most prestigious awards for batsmen in the league.

Notable Winner

David Warner has won the Orange Cap thrice, making him one of the most successful IPL batsmen.

Purple Cap

The Purple Cap is given to the highest wicket-taker in the IPL each season. It symbolizes bowling excellence in the league.

Notable Winner

Bhuvneshwar Kumar has won the Purple Cap twice for Sunrisers Hyderabad.

More Information
Most Valuable Player

The Most Valuable Player (MVP) award is presented to the cricketer who has made the most impact during the IPL season. Points are awarded for runs, wickets, catches, and more.

Notable Winner

Andre Russell won the MVP in IPL 2019 for his all-around performance.

Fair Play Award

The Fair Play Award is given to the team that has displayed the best conduct on the field throughout the IPL season.

Notable Team

Chennai Super Kings have won the Fair Play Award multiple times.

More Information
Super Striker Award

The Super Striker Award is presented to the player with the highest strike rate during the IPL season.

Notable Winner

Andre Russell is known for consistently winning this award with a strike rate over 180.

Emerging Player Award

The Emerging Player Award is given to the best young talent (under 23 years old) in the IPL.

Notable Winner

Shubman Gill won the Emerging Player Award in IPL 2019 for his consistent performances.

Power Player Award

The Power Player Award is presented to the best performer during the powerplay overs of the IPL.

Notable Winner

KL Rahul is known for his aggressive performances in the powerplay and has been a consistent contender for this award.
