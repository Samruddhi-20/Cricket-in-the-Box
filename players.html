MAHENDRA SINGH DHONI

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, born July 7, 1981, is a legendary Indian cricketer known for his role as a wicketkeeper-batsman and captain. A right-handed batsman, Dhoni led India to victory in the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup and the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup. He is celebrated for his calm demeanor and strategic brilliance.

Awards

2008- Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award by Government of India.
2009- Fourth highest civilian award Padma Shri.
2018- Third highest civilian award Padma Bhushan
